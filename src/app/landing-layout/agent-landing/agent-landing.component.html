Managing multiple properties, landlords, tenants & trades is difficult, but having the right software to manage your property can make all the difference. Your Ownly solution is here! Ownly is your ultimate cloud based property management tool. We have revolutionised the way property managers communicate. Our hub provides an integrated multi-user platform for connecting property managers, landlords, tenants and trades all under one roof.

Ownly unique proposition: Property fingerprint

Never lose track of which trades previously worked on a property address. All tradesmen get fingerprinted to every property addresses they work on allowing you to recall them for work with a simple click of a button.

Integrated Communication Platform

No more sorting through hundreds of emails, text messages or dealing with delayed maintenance requests. Everything goes though the Ownly portal, even instant messages!

All communication to landlords, tenants and trades is automatically recorded in your communication log and is attached to the property address so you will never lose track of any communication even when property managers leave and new members join the team, they can easily access all work history against any property within a matter of seconds.

Log and view maintenance requests, history and stage of work performed on any property address and lease start and end dates.

Automated Alerts

Receive automated alerts when a lease agreement is about to come to an end. This allows for sufficient time to arrange a lease renewal and notify tenants of any rent adjustments.

Real Time Communication

Chat to landlords, tenants and trades in real time and never lose track of any communication.

Aggregate View

As a principal or manager, get an aggregate view of how your team is tracking with all their various requests and tasks. If one team member is flooded with requests - you can easily re-assign and share the workload amongst other team members.

Ownly Landlords

Comprehensively caters for one owner with multiple properties or one property owned by multiple owners.

Landlords can log into the system using their own unique username and password and can view any in progress or completed maintenance requests, active leases, download tenancy agreements and communicate with their property managers through real time messaging.

Ownly Tenants

Current tenants can register and view their tenancy status including lease start and expiry dates, keep track of any maintenance requests and maintain easy communication with property managers.
